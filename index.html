Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical website development timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, following our proven 5-phase development process. Complex e-commerce or custom functionality may require additional time. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including 24/7 monitoring, security updates, performance optimization, and content updates. Our support team is available via phone, email, and chat with guaranteed response times.

How do you handle website security?

Security is built into every layer of our development process. We implement SSL encryption, regular security audits, automated backups, and advanced firewall protection. All sites include real-time threat monitoring.

What's included in your SEO optimization?

Every website includes technical SEO optimization, mobile-first indexing, schema markup, and performance optimization. We also provide keyword research, content strategy, and monthly SEO reports.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including monthly installments and pay-as-you-go plans. Projects typically require a 50% deposit, with the balance due upon completion.
